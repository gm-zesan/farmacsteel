Company Introduction

FAR-MAC STEEL INDUSTRY LTD. was invested and constructed by the Indonesia Yuanda Steel Group with an investment of 150 million US dollars, and its annual designed production capacity is 750,000 tons. The group has been deeply engaged in the steel industry for 35 years. It has successively invested in more than ten steel enterprises in countries such as Indonesia, China, Thailand, the Philippines, and Bangladesh.

The group's factory in Bangladesh is located in the Mirsarai Industrial Zone, Chittagong, covering an area of 50 acres. The company has more than 1,000 employees, including dozens of senior experts in the steel industry, quality control experts, raw material procurement experts, and more than 100 steelmaking and steel rolling technical engineers.

Product Series

Building Steel

01

BDS ISO 6935-2 FMS B500DWR

Hot-rolled ribbed steel bars, also known as deformed steel bars, usually have a circular cross-section. There are two longitudinal ribs and evenly distributed transverse ribs on the surface. The cross-section of the transverse ribs is crescent-shaped and does not intersect with the longitudinal ribs. The raw materials are mostly carbon structural steel or low-alloy structural steel, and they are mainly used as the framework of reinforced concrete building components.

02

Coiled deformed steel bar

Coiled deformed steel bar is a type of deformed steel bar, which is in a coiled shape. Its surface is similar to that of the deformed steel bar, with spiral ribs. The cross-section is usually circular, and there are both longitudinal and transverse ribs on the surface to increase the bond strength with the concrete.

03

Angle Steel

Angle steel is widely used in various fields such as construction, machinery manufacturing, and power engineering. In building structures, it is used to fabricate frameworks, supports, ladders, etc. of steel structures. In machinery manufacturing, it can serve as the supporting structure for mechanical parts. In power engineering, it is often used in the manufacturing of transmission towers, etc.

04

Channel Steel

Channel steel is widely used in various fields such as construction, machinery manufacturing, and vehicle manufacturing. In construction projects, it is often used to construct the roof trusses of steel structures, the supporting structures of bridges, the frameworks of buildings, etc. In machinery manufacturing, it can serve as the bases and supports of equipment. In vehicle manufacturing, it is used to fabricate the supporting structures of vehicle frames and carriages, etc.

05

H-beam and I-beam

H-beam and I-beam: With a reasonable cross-sectional shape, excellent mechanical properties and strong load-bearing capacity, they are widely used in projects such as building steel structures, large-scale factory buildings, and bridge construction. They can effectively save the amount of steel used and shorten the construction period.

Product Series

Industrial Steel

01

Steel strip

Strip steel, also known as steel strip, can be classified into high-quality strip steel and ordinary strip steel according to the quality of the steel. According to the rolling method, it is divided into two types: hot-rolled strip steel and cold-rolled strip steel. Strip steel can be manufactured from various steel grades such as plain carbon steel, carbon structural steel, spring steel, tool steel, and stainless steel, and is widely used in the production of welded pipes, hoops, gaskets, spring leaves, saw blades, cutting blades, etc.

02

Flange plate

As a key supporting material for the production of H-shaped steel, precise dimensions and stable quality ensure perfect combination with various types of steel, enhance the overall stability of the structure, and are an indispensable basic material for large-scale projects.

03

Wire rod

After special processing, the surface is smooth, the strength is high, the elongation is good, and it is a high-quality raw material for producing wire and cable products, providing a solid guarantee for cable manufacturing, mechanical processing and other fields.

04

Embankment board

Strong structure, excellent waterproof performance, easy installation, can quickly build an effective water barrier, play an important role in water conservancy projects, bridge foundation construction, river channel rectification and other projects.

05

Rail steel

Track steel is widely used in various fields such as railway main lines, high-speed railways, and urban rail transit. The performance requirements for rail steel vary in different railway scenarios. For example, high-speed railways require rail steel to have higher smoothness and fatigue resistance.
