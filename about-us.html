About Us

FAR-MAC STEEL INDUSTRY LTD. was invested and constructed by the Indonesia Yuanda Steel Group with an investment of 150 million US dollars, and its annual designed production capacity is 750,000 tons. The group has been deeply engaged in the steel industry for 35 years. It has successively invested in more than ten steel enterprises in countries such as Indonesia, China, Thailand, the Philippines, and Bangladesh.

The group's factory in Bangladesh is located in the Mirsarai Industrial Zone, Chittagong, covering an area of 50 acres. The company has more than 1,000 employees, including dozens of senior experts in the steel industry, quality control experts, raw material procurement experts, and more than 100 steelmaking and steel rolling technical engineers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of steel do you supply?

We supply a wide range of steel products, including mild steel, stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel, and galvanized steel. Whether you need steel sheets, bars, coils, plates, or structural sections, we can provide materials that meet your project’s exact specifications.

Do you offer custom steel fabrication services?

Yes. Our custom fabrication services include cutting, bending, welding, drilling, and surface finishing. We work closely with clients to deliver precise, project-specific solutions for construction, manufacturing, and industrial applications.

How do you ensure the quality of your steel products?

Quality is at the core of everything we do. Every batch of steel undergoes rigorous testing and inspection procedures, including tensile strength, composition, and surface integrity tests. Our materials comply with national and international quality standards (ISO, ASTM, EN) to ensure reliability and consistency.

Can you deliver steel products to my location?

Yes. We offer nationwide and international delivery through our reliable logistics network. Depending on your order size and destination, we ensure safe, on-time delivery with proper packaging and handling.

Do you provide material test certificates (MTC)?

Absolutely. We provide Material Test Certificates (MTCs) and other relevant documentation with every order, ensuring full traceability and transparency of the steel’s quality and origin.

Do you support sustainable and eco-friendly steel solutions?

Yes. We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, including recycling processes, energy-efficient production, and sourcing eco-friendly materials. Our goal is to reduce environmental impact while maintaining high-quality steel production.

Why should I choose your company for my steel requirements?

We combine premium-quality products, competitive pricing, expert support, and timely delivery. Our reputation is built on reliability, customer satisfaction, and a deep understanding of the steel industry — making us a trusted partner for projects of all sizes.
