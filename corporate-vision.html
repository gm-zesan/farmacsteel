Our Vision

Shaping the Future of Steel with Innovation, Quality & Global Excellence

FAR-MAC STEEL INDUSTRY LTD., established with an investment of 150 million USD by the Indonesia Yuanda Steel Group, represents a powerful force in Bangladesh’s industrial transformation. With an annual designed capacity of 750,000 tons, our commitment extends beyond manufacturing — we forge progress, sustainability, and innovation.

Supported by over 35 years of experience in the steel industry, Yuanda Group has invested in more than ten enterprises across Asia — including Indonesia, China, Thailand, the Philippines, and Bangladesh. Our factory, located in the Mirsarai Industrial Zone, Chittagong, spans 50 acres and employs more than 1,000 professionals from across the globe — engineers, metallurgists, and quality control experts united by a single vision.

FAR-MAC aims to be a leader in advanced steel technology, driving the future of sustainable infrastructure through innovation, efficiency, and integrity.

  • Global presence with a commitment to regional growth and collaboration
  • Cutting-edge technology and high-efficiency steelmaking processes
  • Empowering a workforce of over 1,000 experts and innovators
  • Committed to sustainability and environmentally responsible production
  • Dedicated to delivering quality steel that builds nations
